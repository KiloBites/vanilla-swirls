On the Subject of Morse Complication

These wires aren't like the others. They have an antiquated form of naval communication! That makes them completely different. The good news is we've found a concise set of instructions, so pay attention and you might just learn something.

The six LEDs at the top of the module transmit Morse code.
However, all transmissions have been obfuscated.
